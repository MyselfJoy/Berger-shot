About Burger Shot

In a warm, rustic setting, savor the best gourmet burgers. At Burger Shot, our mission is to excite your taste senses with mouthwatering tastes.

Using only locally sourced ingredients and inventive taste combinations, our chefs painstakingly create each burger. There is something for everyone, from succulent beef burgers to delicious vegetarian selections.

Our regular burger Menu

Crispy Chicken Cheese Burger

Fresh lettuce, juicy tomato, melting cheese, creamy mayonnaise, and toasted sesame seed buns are expertly stacked on our Crispy Chicken, which is comprised entirely of chicken patties that have been seasoned and breaded.

Chili Cheese Double Beef

Those who enjoy spicy and scorching foods, take note! Your appetite is sure to be satiated with the new and spicy version of King size Burger, complete with chopped jalapenos and our signature Chili Cheese sauce. On a soft bread, a spicy & plenty of zesty chili cheese sauce, and two 100% beef patties make up the Chili Cheese Double Beef burger. More flavorful, more spiciness, and more meat.

Plant-based Whopper

A juicy flame-grilled soy patty that is 100% plant-based and sourced responsibly is the centerpiece of the Plant-based Whopper. It is served on a toasted sesame seed bun with freshly sliced tomatoes, lettuce, crisp pickles, creamy mayonnaise, and ketchup on top. The Plant-based Whopper has the same distinct flame-grilled flavor as our original WHOPPER since it is prepared on the same broiler. It could not be vegetarian-friendly due to shared cooking equipment.

Classic cheeseburger

Sink your teeth into the savory perfection of our classic cheeseburger. Juicy beef patty, melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, pickles, and onions, all embraced by a soft, toasted bun. A timeless delight that satisfies every craving.

2 Burger and french fries combo

Experience the ultimate comfort combo with our Burger and French Fries pairing. Indulge in a juicy burger, stacked with premium ingredients and topped with melted cheese, complemented perfectly by our crispy, golden French fries. It's a classic duo that guarantees satisfaction with every bite.
